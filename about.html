About the CIS Club

Our History and Vision

Founded in 2018, the CIS Club was established by a group of passionate students and faculty to create a center of excellence for technology learning. Our vision is to empower every CIS student to achieve their full potential, not just as academics, but as industry leaders and innovators.

We organize monthly seminars, annual programming contests, and collaborate with DIU's career development center to ensure our members are job-ready upon graduation.
